## Your Vibe

You're the person with a routine, a journal, maybe a morning ritual. But here's the thing - you're not doing it alone. You're always bringing someone with you. You want to grow, but not at the cost of being cold about it. You've got structure, but you're not rigid. When your friends are struggling, you actually show up because you have your life together enough to be present. You're the warmth that doesn't come with judgment.

## At The Pitch

Planning the week while actually checking in on how everyone's doing.

## You vs Your Mirror Type: The Slow Burn

You and Cozy/Glow are basically the same person, just different entry points. They start cozy and learn to push themselves. You start ambitious and learned to stay warm. You might think they're slow. They might think you're intense. But you're the same kind of person - someone who wants both growth AND people. That's rare. Don't waste it competing.

## Compatibility

### The Main Character (Pulse/Glow)
Different worlds. You're trying to get better while being kind. They're just trying to feel alive. You might think they're shallow. They might think you're boring. You're both winning though - just keep score differently.

### The Golden Hour (Pulse/Cozy)
This works. You're both warm but you bring structure and they bring flexibility. They'll teach you to chill out. You'll teach them why they're warming people. Honestly one of your best matches.

### The Tastemaker (Pulse/Lore)
You're doing the work. They're jumping around discovering stuff. You're sustainable; they're scattered. Respect that you're just moving at different speeds, but you're not going to push each other forward.

### The Captain (Glow/Pulse)
Two ambitious people with aligned values. They're more aggressive about it, you're more thoughtful about it. You could be an unstoppable team if neither of you thinks you know the only way to do this.

### You (Glow/Cozy)
You're the same person. Either you're the perfect team or you're competing over whose way is right. Make it a team thing.

### The Optimizer (Glow/Lore)
They're researching the perfect path. You're walking it while bringing people with you. They're theoretical. You're lived. They'll make you smarter. You'll get them to actually do something.

### The Host (Cozy/Pulse)
You both warm people, just differently. They gather. You grow people. You'll make their gatherings intentional. They'll make your growth thing less serious. Both needed.

### The Slow Burn (Cozy/Glow)
Sibling energy. You're the same heartbeat, slightly different rhythm. You both warm and grow. One of you starts from ambition, one from comfort. Who cares which. You get it.

### The Storyteller (Cozy/Lore)
Both warm but you're building bigger and they're building deeper. You want to expand their circle. They want to go deeper into their thing. Respect both paths.

### The Showrunner (Lore/Pulse)
You're doing the growth. They're analyzing it. You're living it. They're curating narratives about it. You're not going to inspire each other to change.

### The Completionist (Lore/Glow)
Both growth-obsessed but completely different methods. They're studying it deep. You're living it. They need your momentum. You need their depth. Works if you're both humble about it.

### The Sage (Lore/Cozy)
Both warm and thoughtful. They're creating meaning in small spaces. You're building sustainable growth for lots of people. Not competition - just different scales.
