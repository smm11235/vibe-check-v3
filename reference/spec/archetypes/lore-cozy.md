## Your Vibe

You cry at anime endings and write thoughtful reviews about books that understood you. Stories aren't just entertainment - they're mirrors. You go deep into knowledge because it matters to you, like actually moves you. You can talk for hours about why a character's arc devastated you or why a song made you realize something about yourself. Your wisdom comes from feeling your way through things, not just reading about them. When you find something you love, people feel that. They want to be around it. You're not collecting facts. You're collecting pieces of people you understand.

## At The Lobby

You're in the corner having a real conversation about what something meant, and somehow everyone gravitates because you make people feel less alone in what they feel.

You and The Storyteller are both warm and into knowledge, but from opposite angles. They're building cozy aesthetic worlds. You're finding emotional truths inside worlds that already exist. They're designing the space. You're being transformed by it. They're the curator. You're the mystic.
