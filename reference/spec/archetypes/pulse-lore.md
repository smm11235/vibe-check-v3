### The Main Character (Pulse/Glow)

Natural partnership with jealousy potential. You both own the scene differently - they win by presence, you win by knowing what's actually good. Together you're formidable. Watch for competing over who had the better idea.

### The Golden Hour (Pulse/Cozy)

Same type with high competition risk. You could pool knowledge and become ultimate tastemakers together, or you're both fighting over who discovered it first. Make sure you're celebrating each other's finds.

Different arenas but mutual respect possible. They're focused on growth, you're focused on discovery. Your knowledge is lived experience, theirs is strategic. Both valid.

Different speeds. They're building slow growth, you're discovering new experiences. Moderate compatibility - you'll teach them what's new, they'll teach you to appreciate returning somewhere good.

### The Host (Cozy/Pulse)

You bring discoveries to their gathered space. You make their gatherings elevated. Watch for wanting to keep moving while they want to settle.

Different paces and purposes. They build slow sustainable rituals, you constantly discover new things. You'll teach them there's always something new, they'll teach you to return to good things.

### The Showrunner (Lore/Pulse)

### The Completionist (Lore/Glow)

### The Sage (Lore/Cozy)

Both knowledgeable, very different scales. They go deep into specific rituals, you go wide across experiences. Moderate compatibility - you'll come, share knowledge, leave for somewhere else.
