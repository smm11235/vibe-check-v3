## Your Vibe

You're the friend who somehow makes self-improvement look chill. You do yoga, you read, you're slowly making your apartment actually nice - but none of it feels like grinding. You just live intentionally and people notice. You're comfortable with where you are right now AND excited about where you're going. That's rare. You get that growth doesn't mean chaotic energy. It means deepening your roots while reaching up. People feel calm around you because you are calm. But also together.

---

## At The Hearth

You're the one holding the wine, suggesting next month's hiking trip, and somehow being both the most relaxed and most put-together person in the room.

---

## You vs Your Mirror Type: The Anchor

You and The Anchor both live the comfort-and-growth thing, but differently. They're intentional about it - like, they decided "I'm improving." You just naturally stumble into growth because the things you like happen to make you better. They're mapping the journey. You're following the good vibes. Both get you to the same place, just different routes.

## You Click With
- People who get that patience is a strategy - not laziness
- Anyone building something slowly and watching it compound
- Folks who don't see comfort and ambition as opposites

## You Clash With

## Compatibility

### The Main Character (Pulse/Glow)
They're sprinting. You're on a lifetime hike. They'll love your steady vibe, but might find you slow. Real synergy if you both respect the different timelines - they keep you moving, you remind them to actually enjoy it.

### The Golden Hour (Pulse/Cozy)
Same comfort energy, different expressions. They just radiate warmth; you cultivate it. You'll click on the coziness, but make sure you're growing in the same direction. No point being cozy if you're going different places.

### The Tastemaker (Pulse/Lore)
They know all the things. You're living all the rituals. They can elevate your life with taste, you can make their knowledge actually mean something. This works.

### The Captain (Glow/Pulse)
They're intense in ways that might stress you. But here's the thing - they need you. You remind them that the destination isn't the whole point. There's real value if you're both willing to learn from each other.

### The Anchor (Glow/Cozy)
This is literally your mirror. You'll understand each other immediately. The risk: you both might expect the other to lead. Talk about growth plans together instead of just assuming it happens naturally.

### The Optimizer (Glow/Lore)
They optimize everything. You just do what feels good. They might think your way is messy; you might think theirs misses the point. Both paths work - agree to disagree gracefully.

### The Host (Cozy/Pulse)
They create spaces. You create rhythms in those spaces. You're kindred on comfort but different on focus. Their events are where you do your best thinking. That's beautiful.

### You (Cozy/Glow)
Same type - you either build something amazing together or you both wait for the other to make the first move. Make sure you're actually pushing each other forward, not just vibing in comfortable silence forever.

### The Storyteller (Cozy/Lore)
You both love cozy. They bring taste, you bring peace. You could literally spend whole evenings just vibing about books and candles. Just stay curious so you don't get too insular.

### The Showrunner (Lore/Pulse)
They're organizing, you're deepening. They move fast and social; you move deep and internal. That friction is actually good for both of you if you respect the difference.

### The Completionist (Lore/Glow)
They want mastery. You want peace. Different languages, same goal. You both care about becoming - just through different routes. There's respect possible here.

### The Sage (Lore/Cozy)
This pairing hits different. They bring emotional wisdom, you bring steady presence. You'll actually feel understood. Just make sure you're pushing each other to stay open, not just retreating into comfort together.
