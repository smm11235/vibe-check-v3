## Compatibility

### The Main Character (Pulse/Glow)
Different games. They're playing to feel alive. You're playing to win. You think they're undisciplined. They think you're overthinking. You're both winners though. Just respect their way is valid even if it's not systematic.

### The Golden Hour (Pulse/Cozy)
Very different operating systems. You're data. They're intuition. You're goals. They're people. You might seem cold. They might seem inefficient. You could learn from each other but you won't naturally vibe.

### The Tastemaker (Pulse/Lore)
Natural allies. You're both knowledgeable but using it differently. They're experiencing. You're strategizing. You'll think they're unsystematic. They'll think you're overthinking. But you're complementary. Together you're dangerous.

### The Captain (Glow/Pulse)
Both ambitious but different methods. They're relational. You're analytical. They might think you're too slow. You might think they're unrigorous. But you're both actually driven. You need each other's strengths.

### The Anchor (Glow/Cozy)
Different speeds and vibes. You're optimizing. They're optimizing with warmth. You might seem cold. They might seem inefficient. But you could teach each other something real.

### You (Glow/Lore)
Same type. You could be unstoppable research and execution together. Or you could compete over who's more optimized. Make sure you're celebrating each other, not competing.

### The Host (Cozy/Pulse)
Very different frequencies. You're analytical and goal-focused. They're relational and present. They might see your optimizing as cold. You might see their gathering as wasteful. Probably not a natural fit.

### The Slow Burn (Cozy/Glow)
Both growth-oriented but different philosophies. You're strategically optimizing everything. They're building sustainable practices with care. You might seem obsessive. They might seem slow. You're both trying to improve, just differently.

### The Storyteller (Cozy/Lore)
Both knowledge-loving but different applications. You're using knowledge to optimize. They're using it to create meaning. You might find them impractical. They might find you shallow. Different orientations.

### The Showrunner (Lore/Pulse)
Both knowledge-obsessed but different expressions. They love research for its own sake. You love what research does. You might feel like they're stuck analyzing. They might feel like you're missing the beauty. You need each other though.

### The Completionist (Lore/Glow)
Sibling energy with complementary strengths. You're both obsessed with mastery and learning. They're pursuing it as an end. You're pursuing it as a means. You could be a powerhouse if neither dismisses the other's relationship to the knowledge.

### The Sage (Lore/Cozy)
Both knowledgeable but different temperaments. You're strategic and analytical. They're reflective and intimate. You might seem calculated. They might seem like they're avoiding action. Different planes.
