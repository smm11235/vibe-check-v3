# Vibe Check: Archetypes Overview
*Four primary archetypes, twelve combo types, six mirror pairs*

---

## 1. Core Principle

**Your primary** = what you're about. **Your secondary** = your flavour.

That's it. Primary is your arena, secondary is your tool. A Pulse/Lore person shows up socially but brings knowledge. A Lore/Pulse person gathers knowledge but presents it to groups. Same DNA, different energy.

**Lore**  - The depth. You go deep on the things you care about. Obsession is your love language, and you respect anyone who does the same.

## 4. Mirror Type Pairs

Mirror types are the same two archetypes flipped. Same vibe, different emphasis.

| Mirror Pair | Key Difference |
|-------------|---------------|
| Main Character - Captain | Main Character leads with charisma; Captain leads with effort |
| Golden Hour - Host | Golden Hour brings warmth to rooms; Host creates warmth in their space |
| Tastemaker - Showrunner | Tastemaker absorbs knowledge effortlessly; Showrunner geeks out and builds community |
| Anchor - Slow Burn | Anchor grows through conscious effort; Slow Burn grows through cozy routines |
| Optimizer - Completionist | Optimizer researches the best path; Completionist dives deep because they love mastering it |
| Storyteller - Sage | Storyteller curates aesthetic worlds; Sage finds emotional wisdom in what they explore |
